ART WILL SAVE US.Our art practice follows a unique cycle: order, chaos, disorder -- the inception point of our art. As collaborators, we believe that art lives in every pocket of human existence. We celebrate all forms of art: the art of community, the art of mess, the art of unclear ideas, the art of nature. At its core, our practice is a celebration of life and human nature, grounded in the belief that there is beauty -- and value -- in the inevitable return to disorder.
